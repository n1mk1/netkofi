// our mission

We built NetKofi because
networking is broken.

Every week you meet brilliant people — at conferences, coffee chats, hackathons, happy hours. You add them on LinkedIn. And then you never talk again.

It's not that you don't care. It's that nothing reminds you to follow up. No one holds you accountable. And without momentum, connections fade into a list of names you can barely place.

NetKofi is the app that closes that gap. We track who you've met, nudge you to follow up, and show you your real network — not the one you collected, but the one you actually maintain. Your relationships, made visible.
